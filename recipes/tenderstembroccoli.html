Sauteed Tenderstem Broccoli with Garlic

Ingredients

  • 250 g Tenderstem broccoli
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic thinly sliced
  • 0.5 lemon juice and zest
  • salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Heat a large frying pan to medium-high and add the olive oil.
  2. Remove any tough stems then add the broccoli to the pan and cook on a high heat for 2-3 minutes, tossing once or twice as it cooks.
  3. Add the garlic and cook for another minute, tossing to ensure the garlic doesn't catch or burn.
  4. Squeeze over the lemon juice and let it evaporate before turning off the heat.
  5. Season with salt and plenty of pepper and toss to combine.
  6. Serve with the lemon zest sprinkled over the top.
